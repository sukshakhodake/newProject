Our Approach

We approach preschool education with all the seriousness and sanctity that it warrants without taking away the enjoyment of the learning process. Today’s children need experiential learning where every sense comes into play for optimal development. With our philosophy of ‘Every child’s every aspect is important’, the school focuses on the all-round development of each student.  

While keeping in mind that each child is different, every child will be nurtured to excel academically as well as encouraged to develop their talents in art, music, sports and other extra curricular pursuits. 

Our program covers six areas of development:

  • Personal, Social and Emotional Development
  • Language Development
  • Creative Development
  • Physical Development
  • Mathematical Understanding
  • Knowledge and Understanding of the World

In today's knowledge-led society, The Weavers Preschool is committed to the passion of imparting education that empowers.

Environment

  • Child-friendly classrooms – for safe movement.
  • Safe and secure environment with CCTV cameras – to learn without fear.
  • Caring and nurturing teachers – to joyfully guide the learning process.
  • Large outdoor play area – for gross motor development, balance and co-ordination.
  • Sand play and water play – for exploration, discovery and learning scientific concepts along with fine motor skills and tactile development.
  • Plant nursery – for nurturing life and environmental awareness.
  • Reading corner and role-play areas – for creativity and imagination.
  • Art area – for emotional and aesthetic development andcreative expression.
  • Music and movement – for finer brain development and understanding rhythm and beat.
