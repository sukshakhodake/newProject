Our Team

Mansi Shah

Founder

The founder and brainchild behind The Weavers Preschool, Mansi did her BA in Economics from University of California at San Diego, USA and later Masters in Economics from University of Manchester, UK.

After working in various corporates like Johnson & Johnson in Singapore and Alliance World in Chandigarh she found her real calling - teaching. She taught at Tridha –a Waldorf school which is one of the most alternative schools of Mumbai. Tridha taught her a lot about the education industry and thus she decided to start her own venture in education – The Weavers Preschool along with her husband Kaivalya Shah.

Kaivalya fully supports her dream of creating an international standard preschool that will impart knowledge that is beyond textbooks and learning that is beyond classrooms. They believe that kids should play, have fun, enjoy and while they are at it, learning happens.

Purnima Shetye

Mentor

The mentor, teacher trainer and curriculum developer for The Weavers Preschool, Purnima has over 36 years of experience in the field of education. She has completed her Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Education from the University of Poona – Pune. She has also successfully completed a TEFL – Teaching English as Foreign Language course.

She started her journey as an educator soon after she completed her graduation in 1978. Since then she has taught all levels – from Pre-Nursery to Grade XII. Her vast experience from various schools and institutions in Nasik, PCMC and Pune, helped her successfully set up National and International pre-schools, Child Care and Activity Centres and a CBSE Grade-Level school.

She combines leadership qualities with innate people skills, management skills and excellent communication and interpersonal skills. As a principal of National and International pre-schools, she has developed and conducted various training sessions for schools besides  facilitating  numerous workshops for parents and students on many child-centric topics along with Professional Development programs for teachers.

Chetan Shah

Advisor

Chetan Shah, is a B. Tech in Civil Engineering from IIT Bombay and M.S in Structural Engineering from the University of Houston, USA. People who know him say that he can give most mathematicians a run for their money. He has mentored various start-ups from a furniture company to a steel trading company to a stock market brokerage company. He is advising Mansi and Kaivalya to make this venture a success and take this venture pan Maharashtra.

 Chetan Shah has been on the board of SMPR School, a K12 school in Mulund and is often called in the prestigious Welingkar Institute of Management as a guest lecturer.
