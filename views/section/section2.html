About Us

I moved to Mumbai from Chandigarh a few years ago. You fall in love with the city, it's opportunities and life style conditions, but somewhere along the way space gets compromised. As grown-ups we can squeeze through these tight spaces without it affecting us much. However, for a child to grow up in a boxed up spaces can limit their thinking and imagination capacity. In my opinion, no playground is big enough for a child. No space is space enough for them to grow and learn. I believe that there is no greated lesson than the ones you learn outdoors
